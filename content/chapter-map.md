# Content Chapter Map
*The master story architecture for Ro & Cody's fertility journey.*
*Updated: April 2026*

---

## Two Channels

**Channel 1 — Ro-Forward (Personal Brand)**
Ro leads. First-person, emotional, real-time journey. Cody appears as supporting cast.
Homes: @roco_babyquest Instagram, web /our-story, email narrative sequence.

**Channel 2 — Data/Faceless**
No faces. Voiceover + text + graphics. Attention-grabbing stats and policy angles.
Fills the gaps when Channel 1 isn't ready to publish.
Homes: YouTube longform, Instagram Reels cuts, infographic posts.

---

## Channel 1 Chapters — The Journey

### Chapter 1: "The Dream"
*Who Ro is before the struggle starts.*

- **Core:** 12 years as a nanny. She has loved other people's children her whole career. Every time she leaves for the day, the quiet hope follows her home.
- **Hook:** "I've helped raise kids for 12 years. I just want one of my own."
- **Emotion:** Warmth, longing, identity
- **Formats:** Instagram intro post, web /our-story opener
- **Data tie-in:** "1 in 8 couples faces infertility — including us."
- **Status:** Ready. Content exists in recipient-story.md.

---

### Chapter 2: "When It Didn't Just Happen"
*The quiet shift from hoping to realizing they needed help.*

- **Core:** The decision to seek medical help. The vulnerability of admitting your body isn't doing what you expected.
- **Hook:** "At some point, hoping turns into asking for help."
- **Emotion:** Vulnerability, courage, the weight of the decision
- **Formats:** Instagram carousel, web story section
- **Data tie-in:** Average time to diagnosis. Most couples wait 12+ months before seeking care.
- **Status:** Ro determines timing and depth.

---

### Chapter 3: "The IUI Gauntlet"
*Four cycles. The hope, the math, the waiting, the failure.*

- **Core:** Four rounds of IUI over multiple months. The emotional cycle of injections → waiting → negative. The data reality: 10–20% per cycle.
- **Hook:** "We did everything right. Four times."
- **Emotion:** Determination, exhaustion, dark humor about the process
- **Formats:** Multi-part Instagram series (one post per cycle), web timeline (already built), faceless video segment
- **Data tie-in:** IUI success rates, cumulative failure odds, cost per cycle (~$2K each)
- **Status:** Ready. Full timeline already live at /our-story. Ro decides how much detail to share on Instagram.
- **Note:** Cycles 1–2 are straightforward. Cycle 4 ("the zombie cycle") has a powerful authentic angle.

---

### Chapter 4: "The Worst Kind of Good News"
*The miscarriage at cycle 3 — a pregnancy that ended at 6 weeks.*

- **Core:** They got pregnant. It worked. Then it was gone. The specific grief of losing a pregnancy you fought to get is different.
- **Hook:** "We got the positive test. Then we lost it."
- **Emotion:** Grief, the particular loneliness of this kind of loss
- **Formats:** Single Instagram post (when ready), web story section
- **Data tie-in:** 15–25% miscarriage rate after fertility treatment — more common than people know.
- **Status:** Ro controls timing entirely. Do not push. May be the most powerful post when it comes.

---

### Chapter 5: "Running the Math"
*The moment IVF becomes the only path and the cost becomes real.*

- **Core:** IVF is next. $16K+ per cycle, plus medications. You can do the math in your head and it doesn't work. That's when BabyQuest entered the picture.
- **Hook:** "The treatment exists. We just couldn't afford it without help."
- **Emotion:** Fear, pragmatism, the unfairness of it
- **Formats:** Instagram post + data callout, crossover with Channel 2 (cost breakdown content)
- **Data tie-in:** $16K cycle cost, 85% paid out of pocket, how many cycles the average couple needs
- **Status:** Ready.

---

### Chapter 6: "The Call"
*April 6, 2026 — the grant news.*

- **Core:** Finding out they were selected as Spring 2026 BabyQuest recipients. Sitting with the news before knowing what to say.
- **Hook:** "We sat with it for a long time before we knew what to say."
- **Emotion:** Gratitude, relief, responsibility to share the story
- **Formats:** Instagram announcement post, web hero moment, NIAW anchor post
- **Data tie-in:** BabyQuest Foundation impact (250+ grants, 215+ babies, $3.9M awarded)
- **Status:** Ready now. This is the NIAW anchor post.

---

### Chapter 7: "What We're Building"
*IVF ahead. The future they're moving toward.*

- **Core:** The grant makes IVF possible. The journey continues. They're sharing it publicly because other couples deserve to know BabyQuest exists.
- **Hook:** "There are other Rochelles and Codys out there."
- **Emotion:** Hope, purpose, community
- **Formats:** Ongoing Instagram series as IVF progresses; evolves in real time
- **Status:** Forward-looking. Develops as the journey unfolds.

## Channel 2 Chapters — Data/Faceless

These stand alone and don't require any personal disclosure. Deploy anytime.

### D1: "Why Does Where You Live Determine If You Can Have a Baby?"
- State mandate gap — 30+ states with no IVF coverage
- Ohio angle: 900 cycles/million vs. 3,200 in New York
- Format: YouTube longform (8–10 min) + 60-sec Reels cut
- Data: mandate map, cycles per million chart, HB 38 update
- Status: Data ready. Script not started. **This is the first faceless video.**

### D2: "What IUI Actually Costs (In Every Way)"
- Not just money — time, hormones, emotional labor, the two-week wait
- Format: Instagram carousel + short Reels
- Data: IUI cost per cycle, cumulative success rates, dropout statistics
- Status: Ready. Pairs with Chapter 3 as cross-channel content.

### D3: "The Grant That Shouldn't Have to Exist"
- BabyQuest origin story + why a nonprofit had to fill this gap
- Format: YouTube mid-length (5–7 min)
- Data: Foundation impact stats, insurance coverage failure rates, who gets left out
- Status: Needs Pamela's blessing on framing.

### D4: "Ohio's Fertility Blind Spot"
- Ohio-specific: mandate with no IVF, HB 38 status, local clinic access
- Format: Instagram infographic series + short video
- Data: Ohio stats already in DB
- Status: Ready. Good local media hook too.

### D5: "The Real Cost of One IVF Cycle"
- Line-item breakdown: retrieval, medications, ICSI, PGT, transfer, cryostorage
- Format: Instagram carousel, YouTube segment
- Data: Widely available; no personal disclosure required
- Status: Ready.
